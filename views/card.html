seasons cards

spring season

In spring, the earth sheds its wintry cloak, Awakening from slumber, nature awoke. Blossoms burst forth in a riot of hues, As life renews, casting off winter's blues.

winter season

In winter's grasp, the world lies still and cold, Wrapped in blankets of snow, a tale of old. Silent woods and frosty air do enthrall, In winter's embrace, a serene, icy thrall.

rainy season

In the rainy season, skies weep with sorrow's embrace, Pattering droplets dance, painting the world's face. Rivers swell, trees sway in the storm's gentle call, Nature's symphony plays, enchanting one and all.

autumn season

In autumn's embrace, leaves dance and swirl, A symphony of colors, a majestic twirl. Cool breezes whisper secrets through the trees, Nature's canvas painted with golden memories.

summer season

Under the summer sun's golden embrace, Fields of green stretch with abundant grace. Laughter and joy fill the warm air, As nature's symphony plays everywhere.
